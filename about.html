ABOUT

I am Sofia Grimblat a 22-year-old graphic design student from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I was born in an artistic family where music and art is the medium to share time together.

I began studying graphic design in 2016 in the University of Buenos Aires and moved to Miami in 2017 that is when I continued studying in the Miami International University.

Graphic Design is my passion. When I was a little kid I played creating my own letters, making collages, painting, drawing. I was already submerged in the visual world. And when I finished high school and I had no doubt that my career was graphic design.

If you observe the streets that you transit every day you see graphic design. You can see it in packaging, street signs. So, for me being a graphic designer is a big responsibility you have to know the culture where the piece will be submerged, investigate the product you have to work with and then start working on the piece.

In this website you will find the pieces that I been working on editorial, posters, product design pieces and will be in currently updated. Hope you enjoy!!
